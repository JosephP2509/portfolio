Animations Page


The image above shows the last scene of the End Of Course Interactive Animation Project. This project features a person who was driving and lost control due ice on the road and crashed into a tree. They end up opening up the engine bay to analyze the damage and get teleported to the back of the tree which had a portal on it. They then decide to go through the portal and end up in a room. In that room they find a bookshelf, when they go to examine the bookshelf they wake up to realize that it was all a dream. In the end they decide to go back to sleep and then the story repeats itself. Click the image above to play the End Of Course Interactive Animation Project.
