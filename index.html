About Me:

I am Joseph Puerto, a dual-enrollment student at Caldwell Applied Sciences Academy (CASA) and Caldwell Community College & Technical Institute (CCCTI). I am graduating with an associate's degree in Graphic Design and Mobile App Development and a high school diploma. I have experience with Adobe programs such as Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, PremierePro, Audition, and Character Animator. I am also experienced in writing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code. My interest in graphic design and mobile app development started in middle school; as my interest grew, I began to look for schools that could offer programs that could help me develop into the skilled individual I am today. Eventually, I found Caldwell Applied Sciences Academy, which allowed me to take the college classes that Caldwell Community College & Technical Institute offered. Throughout my career at CASA, I have created various projects, from a four-by-two-inch prom ticket design to a 48-by-36-inch promotional bulletin board; I even designed my portfolio website from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code.
